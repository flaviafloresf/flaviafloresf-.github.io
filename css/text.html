Text Alignment

  • text-align: (center, left, right or justify) sets the horizontal alignment of a text.
    • center
    • left
    • right
    • To see how justify works we need at least a whole sentence.
  • text-align-last: (center, right or justify) is specific for the last line of text.
  • direction and unicode-bidi: (direction: rtl; unicode-bidi: ()bidi-override;) can be used to change the text direction of an element (mirror)
    • can you read this?
  • unicode-bidi.
  • vertical-align (baseline, text-top, text-bottom, sub, super) sets the vertical alignment of an element.
